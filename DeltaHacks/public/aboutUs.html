About Us

Nourish is a platform that allows for direct communication between a supplier with excess food, whether it be a supermarket, restaurant, or food truck, and someone in need of that food. Nourish aims to be an invisible middle man between a food provider and someone in need. There is an aspect of embarassment and shyness when someone in need has to go ask for food. We want to completely eliminate that.

Nourish was created at DeltaHacks VI by Abdulrahman Hamideh, Khaled Hassan, Abdelrahman Elleithy, and Mahmoud Anklis. We decided to create this platform to combat food waste as well as hunger faced in homeless communities. According to CNET 95% of homeless people own phones. We tried to take advantage of this fact by creating Nourish.

Nourish works by food providers firstly registering their business as one that has excess food and is willing to give it away at the end of the day. The business is then added to a map which someone in need can anonymously access through either the webapp or a mobile app.
